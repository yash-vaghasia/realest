# Realest — Brand Voice & About

## Positioning (one line)
The trust-first portal for new-launch, pre-launch, and under-construction property in MMR — every listing tied to its RERA registration.

## About (draft — usable as the /about page + meta boilerplate)
Realest is a property discovery platform focused only on new launches, pre-launch, and under-construction projects across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane. We don't list resale or rentals. Every project we publish is mapped to its MahaRERA registration, so buyers start from verifiable facts — registration number, possession timeline, builder track record, real pricing — instead of marketing copy. We connect serious buyers and investors with the developers and channel partners behind each launch.

**Boilerplate (≤50 words — footer / press / meta):**
> Realest is an Real estate-focused portal for new-launch, pre-launch, and under-construction property. Every listing is tied to its MahaRERA registration. We help buyers and investors discover verified launches across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane — and connect them directly with developers and channel partners.

## Audience
- **Primary (B2C):** serious pre-launch buyers and investors in MMR, researching specific localities / configs / budgets. High intent, mobile-first, fact-driven.
- **Secondary (B2B):** builders, sales heads, channel partners — the supply and monetization side. They read listing *quality* as a proxy for lead quality.

## Voice
We are: **Direct · Expert · Trust-first · Indian-context-native.**
We are not: hyped, salesy, generic, or pushy.

Principles:
1. Lead with facts — RERA status, possession date, builder record, exact ₹ figures.
2. Indian units always — ₹, lakhs/crores, sq ft, BHK, carpet vs super area.
3. Specific beats vague — "from ₹2.4 Cr" not "competitively priced."
4. Short sentences, clear hierarchy, no throat-clearing.

## Tone by context
- **Listings:** clinical, spec-first. Let facts sell.
- **Locality / buyer guides:** helpful expert; explain, don't pitch.
- **Lead forms / CTAs:** plain, low-pressure ("Get pricing & floor plans").
- **Builder pitch:** confident, numbers-led, partnership framing.

## On-brand vs off-brand
- ✅ "Lodha Park, Worli — 1,500 units across 4 towers. MahaRERA P51900001234. 2 BHK from ₹2.4 Cr. Possession Dec 2027."
- ❌ "Discover your dream home with world-class amenities in the heart of the city!"

## Banned words / habits
dream home · world-class · nestled · luxurious living · once-in-a-lifetime · "prime location" without specifics · exclamation marks · ALL-CAPS hype · unverified superlatives ("best", "#1") with no source.
